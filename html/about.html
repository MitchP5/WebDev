Childhood

I was born in Pleasanton, California, just outside of San Francisco. My parents are from Alberta, Canada and moved down to California so my dad could go to Chiropractic school. I attended Foothill High School and graduated in 2019. I then went on to serve a mission for my church in the Dominican Republic where I was tasked with learning Spanish to be able to teach and serve the Dominican Republic. I returned in 2021 and I am now a student at Brigham Young University.

I am currently in the pre-accounting program at BYU where I plan to get my Masters degree. I am also working towards a minor in Information Systems. I am planning on pursuing a career in sales and real estate.



Hobbies

Growing up, sports were a huge part of my life. It is how I met most of my closest friends and how I bonded with my family. My main sports that I have played throughout High School were football, basketball, and golf. I also really enjoy watching sports, and being form the Bay Area, my favorite teams are the Golden State Warriors, the San Francisco 49ers, and the San Francisco Giants.

I also found peace at the beach and in the water. It could be surfing, wakeboarding, hot tubbing, scuba diving, playing spikeball on the beach, or even just laying out in the sun. The water and the beach are my happy places.
